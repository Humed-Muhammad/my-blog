## 3 Ways to Optimise Your Website SEO

1. Improve your site Metadata
2. Use alt tags in your image
3. Write rich content

## Metadata

One way to improve your website's Google ranking is to make sure that your metadat is in order. Metadata is the information that tells search engines what your website is about. making sure that your metadata is accurate and up-to-date can help improve your ranking.

You can edit your website's metadata by visiting Google's Search Console. There, you can add or change the title, description, and keywords for your website. You can also add images and other files, which will help search engines better understand your website.

Make sure to keep your metadat fresh and accurate, and you'll see an improvement in your Google ranking.

## Metadata Example

```html:Metadata example
<head>
  <meta charset="UTF-8">
  <meta name="description" content="Your site description">
  <meta name="keywords" content="Keywords separated by commas">
  <meta name="author" content="your name">
  <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0"> //To make your website look good on all devices
</head>
  ...
}
```

## alt tags in image

One way to help boost your website's Google rankings is by using alt tags in your images. This is an easy way to tell Google what your images are about, and it can help your site rank higher in search engine results pages.

Make sure to use keywords in your alt tags, as this will help Google better understand the content of your images. And don't forget to keep your alt tags short and concise—Google only displays a limited number of characters in its results pages, so make sure to get the most important information across quickly and clearly.

## Write rich contents

One of the best ways to boost your website's Google rankings is to write rich, engaging content. This means going beyond simply throwing up a few paragraphs of text and slapping some keywords in there.

Instead, take the time to create well-written and well-researched articles that your audience will love. Not only will this help you rank higher on Google, but it will also help you build a loyal following of readers who will come back for more.

## Honorable mentions

There are a lot of factors that go into getting your website ranked high on Google. But thankfully, there are a few simple things you can do to give your website a little boost.

### Optimized for Mobile

Make sure your website is optimized for mobile. More and more people are using their phones and tablets to search for information, so it's important that your website looks good and is easy to navigate on smaller screens.

### Updated Regularly

Make sure your website is updated regularly with fresh content. Google loves websites that are constantly adding new information, so make sure you post new blog articles, images, and videos on a regular basis.

### Social Media

Get involved in social media. Platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram provide great opportunities to share your content with a wider audience and connect with potential customers.

## Conclusion

Google is the biggest search engine in the world, so it's important to make sure your website ranks high on its search results pages.

There are a number of things you can do to optimize your website for Google, and these three tips are a great place to start.

1. Make sure your website is fast and mobile-friendly.

3. Use keywords and phrases throughout your website, including in the title, meta description, and header tags.
